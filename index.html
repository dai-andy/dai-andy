Research

  • This summer, I'm conducting research on cancer immunotherapy. More information coming soon!

  • From last summer to winter, I conducted research on the introduction of malaria-resistant transgenic mosquitoes as a novel proposal for reducing the global impact of malaria. I built systems of ordinary differential equations (ODEs) that modeled the different mosquito populations (eg. hybrid, pure transgenic...) and their population dynamics, and I analyzed them for general results on the stability of a hypothetical environmental system with released transgenic mosquitos.
